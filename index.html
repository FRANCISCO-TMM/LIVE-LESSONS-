Brief biography

I was a student of Holy Family Secondary School, where an early curiosity about how systems break — and how they're defended — set the direction for everything that came after. That curiosity turned into a decision to study cybersecurity formally, and today I'm building the technical foundation (networking, operating systems, programming, and now web development through this very course) that a security specialist needs. Outside the classroom, I run an online learning community and stay close to the practical side of tech through gaming and the DeFi ecosystem.
